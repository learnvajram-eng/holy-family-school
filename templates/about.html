A Word from Our Principal

"Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to Holy Family English Medium School — a place where every child's journey is met with care, encouragement, and a deep commitment to excellence.

At Holy Family, we believe education is far more than the mastery of subjects. It is about forming young people of integrity — individuals who will go out into the world with courage, compassion, and a sense of purpose.

Our motto, Service, Love and Joy, is not merely written on our walls; it lives in the hearts of our teachers, staff and students every single day. We strive to create an environment where learning is joyful, relationships are meaningful, and every child feels they truly belong.

Our Story

History & Legacy

Holy Family English Medium School was established in 1970 by the Franciscan Sisters of the Immaculate Heart of Mary, with a vision to provide quality English-medium education to the children of Sattenapalli and the surrounding region.

From humble beginnings with fewer than 100 students, the school has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in Palnadu District, serving over 1,200 students from LKG to Class X.

Over five decades, we have celebrated thousands of graduates who carry our motto of Service, Love and Joy into every corner of society — as doctors, engineers, teachers, entrepreneurs and public servants.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution that holistically develops young minds — academically excellent, morally strong, socially responsible — inspired by the values of the Holy Family.

Our Mission
  • Provide quality, inclusive English-medium education accessible to all children
  • Foster intellectual curiosity and a lifelong love of learning
  • Develop character, empathy, and ethical leadership in every student
  • Create a joyful, safe learning environment anchored in Service, Love and Joy
  • Equip students with 21st-century skills for a rapidly changing world
